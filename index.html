About FEFO

The FEFO Olympiad celebrates Japan's remarkable post-war revival through education, inspired by Emperor Hirohito's famous question: "How many teachers survived?"

This event brings together junior high students from across Bandung to compete in academic challenges that honor Japan's dedication to learning and excellence.

Competitions

Ronin Science Challenge

Test your scientific knowledge in this rigorous challenge inspired by Japan's research excellence.

Bushido Math Olympiad

Solve complex problems with the discipline and precision of a samurai warrior.

Haiku Writing Contest

Express your creativity through the traditional Japanese poetry form of 5-7-5 syllables.

Origami Engineering

Combine art and engineering principles to create innovative paper structures.

Sumo Robotics

Design and program robots to compete in a miniature sumo wrestling arena.

Ikebana Art Competition

Create beautiful floral arrangements following the principles of Japanese flower arrangement.
